Relaxing
feature
selection
in
spam
filtering

using
case-based
reasoning
systems
This
paper

feature
selection
on
a
well
known
case-based
reasoning
spam
filtering
system
called
SPAMHUNTING
.
We
present
the
usage
of
the
k
more
predictive
features
and
a
percentage-based
strategy
for
the
exploitation
of
our
amount
of
information
measure
.
Finally
,
we
confirm
the
idea
that
the
percentage
feature
selection
method
is
more
adequate
for
spam
filtering
domain
.
